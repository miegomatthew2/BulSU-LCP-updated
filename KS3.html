RECALIBRATING CURRICULAR OFFERINGS AND ASSESSMENT PRACTICES FOR ADOPTION OF THE FLEXIBLE LEARNING MODALITIES (2022)

To recalibrate the curricular offering and practices, faculty members shall be ready enough as they venture to the New Normal this academic year 2022-2023. Further, the University came up with face to face seminar on Capacity Building: Implementation of OBEdized Education on August 16, 2022 and a Webinar on Multidisciplinary Approach in Teaching and Learning on August 18, 2022.
